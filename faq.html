Getting Started

1. Sign Up

Just enter your email address and your phone number with country code. (+1 in the U.S.) We promise not to abuse the privilege.

2. Verify Code

You will be text-messaged a verification code from Texty. Enter the 6 character code into the signup form and your phone number will be confirmed.

3. Enjoy Texty

You can now interact with the full list of commands that Texty offers, straight from SMS! Just text the number you received your verification code from to start controlling your SkyDrive. We recomment starting with the '?' command to get a list of commands. Enjoy!

FAQ

What is Texty?

Texty is an SMS interface to your Microsoft SkyDrive account. It enables you to browse your SkyDrive, share files and more just by sending us a text message! No smart phone required.

How do I use this?

You can text 'hlp' or '?' to get the latest list of commands. Try starting with 'find' and part of the name of a file you want a sharable link to.

I don't want an account anymore, what do I do?

Just text us "unsub" and we'll remove your account. We hope you'll send us feedback.
