Getting Started

1. Sign Up

Input your Microsoft Skydrive email into the email field. Input your phone number into the number field, including a prefixed country code (+1 if you are in the United States)

2. Verify Code

You will be text-messaged a verification code from TexTy. Enter the 6 character code into the validation field and your account will be confirmed.

3. Enjoy TexTy

You may now interact with the full list of commands that TexTy offers, straight from SMS! Just SMS the number you received your verification code from and your commands will execute on your Skydrive. Enjoy!

FAQ

1. What is TexTy?

TexTy is an SMS interface to your Microsoft SkyDrive account. It enables you to execute commands and share files by texting a number from your phone without ever launching a browser!
